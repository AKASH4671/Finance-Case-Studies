# Finance Case Studies Portfolio

This repository contains independent finance case studies analyzing strategic corporate transactions across technology and digital industries. The studies combine financial analysis, valuation methodologies, and industry research to evaluate the strategic and economic implications of major acquisitions.

Author: Akash Ghosal

---

## Case Studies

### Microsoft – Spotify Acquisition Analysis

A strategic and valuation assessment evaluating whether Microsoft should pursue the acquisition of Spotify.

Key areas covered:

* Industry and competitive landscape analysis
* Financial performance evaluation
* EV/Revenue valuation framework
* Strategic synergies and acquisition rationale
* Key risks and integration considerations

---

### NVIDIA – ARM Acquisition Strategic Analysis

A comprehensive analysis examining the strategic feasibility and valuation implications of NVIDIA acquiring Arm in the evolving AI compute landscape.

Key areas covered:

* Global semiconductor and AI compute industry structure
* Review of the failed 2020–2022 NVIDIA–Arm acquisition attempt
* Strategic rationale for vertical integration
* Scenario-based DCF valuation
* Regulatory feasibility assessment
* Risk analysis and mitigation strategies
